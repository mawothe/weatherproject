Summary: City Latitude versus Weather

The purpose of the project was to examine how weather data changes as a city's location nears the equator. First, information was pulled from the OpenWeatherAPI to compile a dataset of over 500 cities.

After gather, compiling and cleaning the data, we then used Matplotlib to create visual representations of what the numbers were saying about various aspects of the weather in compared to the other cities and their latitudes. These factors include wind speed, maximum temperatures, humidity and overall cloudiness. The site provides access to the source data, visualizations graphed during the analysis, and descriptions of the trends shown on the plots.
